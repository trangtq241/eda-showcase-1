Data analysis

Business performance metrics

Gross merchandise value (GMV)

  • Total GMV increased sharply, almost x10 from ~$100k to ~$1000k/ month, in 2017 and stables at ~$900k/ month in the first 8 months of 2018

Average order value (AOV)

  • Similar to GMV, the number of monthly delivered orders also rose by 10 times in 2017, then stablized at ~7000 levels in 2018. The delivery rate was improved considerably from 93% to 98% over 1.5 years

  • The canceled order rates were kept below 1.3%, while the percentage of unavailable orders decreased significantly from 2.5% to around 0.2% recently which means the product availability on the e-commerce platform has met consumers’ demands

  • 50% of orders has their values above $80-$90 and this median AOV stables over 18 months

=> There is a room for AOV improvement by better cross-sell & up-sell

Customer retention rate

  • There are only 1788 returned customer over the last 18 months, making up 1.87%

=> The retention rate is surprisingly low for an e-commerce platform

Operation performance metrics

Order processing time

  • There is a remarkable improvement in order processing time in recent 5 months with the median dropping from ~13 days to ~7 days per order

  • Nov 2017, Feb & Mar 2018 witnessed 10-20% of orders being lately-delivered. However, the situation was quickly rectified.

  • In general, around 95% of orders are delivered on time or early compared to estimation for most of the observed months

Customer behaviours

Customer rating scores

  • The average review scores have been kept above 4, except for months when some late-delivered orders were recorded

Popular product categories

  • The marketplace is most popular for Bed-Bath-Table, Heath-Beauty and Sport-Leisure product categories with highest number of orders in 18 months

  • Watches-Gifts is also a noticeable category for second highest total GMV following Heath-Beauty

  • As for the taste transition trend, there is a surge in popularity of Health-Beauty and Computers-Accessories as a replacement of Sport-Leisure and Furniture Decor in the Top 3

  • Watches-Gifts and Auto categories also occupy the places of Toys and Cool Stuff in the Top 10

Popular order hours

  • The peak daytime for orders are between 10am and 17pm in weekdays.

  • Evenings from Monday to Thursday & Sunday are second popular for online purchase

=> It’s the time when system stability is crucial and campaigns should be run

Popular payment methods

  • Credit card is the most used payment method, especially for high valued orders (around $106)

  • Vouchers are usually paid for low valued orders (around $40)

Insights summary

  • Business performance

    • Both GMV & number of deliverred orders were x10 in 2017, stabling at ~$900k and ~7000 orders/ months respectively in 2018
    • The delivery rate was improved considerably from 93% to 98% over 1.5 years
    • The canceled order rates were constantly kept below 1.3%
    • Percentage of unavailable orders decreased from 2.5% to around 0.2%
    => The product availability on the e-commerce platform has met consumers’ demands
    • 50% of orders has their values above $80-$90 and this median AOV stables over 18 months
    => There is a room for AOV improvement by better cross-sell & up-sell
    • There are only 1788 returned customer over the last 18 months, making up 1.87%

    => The retention rate is surprisingly low for an e-commerce platform

  • Operation performance

    • There is a remarkable improvement in order processing time in recent 5 months with the median dropping from ~13 days to ~7 days per order
    • Nov 2017, Feb & Mar 2018 witnessed 10-20% of orders being lately-delivered. However, the situation was quickly rectified
    • Roughly 95% of orders are delivered on time or early compared to estimation for most of the observed months

  • Customer behaviours

    • The average review scores have been kept above 4, except for months when some late-delivered orders were recorded
    • The marketplace is most popular for Bed-Bath-Table, Heath-Beauty and Sport-Leisure product categories over 18 months
    • Watches-Gifts is also a noticeable category for second highest total GMV following Heath-Beauty
    • There is a surge in popularity of Health-Beauty and Computers-Accessories** to Top 3, and Watches & Gifts and Auto to Top 10
    • The peak daytime for orders are between 10am and 17pm in weekdays. Evenings from Monday to Thursday & Sunday are second popular for online purchase
    => It’s the time when system stability is crucial and campaigns should be run
    • Credit card is the most used payment method, especially for high valued orders, while vouchers are usually paid for low valued ones

Recommendations

  • Enhance cross-sell & up-sell by recommendation engine on the platform

  • Improve customer retention rate to make full use of customer lifetime value by winback campaigns & loyalty programs

  • After late delivery, there should be sorry letters and/ or gifts for customers’ disconsentment relief
